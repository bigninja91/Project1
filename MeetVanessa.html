Meet Vanessa

Vanessa Louise Event and wedding planning is a full service planning boutique specialising in timeless weddings, events and occasions. I gained my experience in fashion and design at the University Of Derby. From this, I have worked as a freelance stylist, personal shopper and image consultant. I have worked with many brides and mother of the brides, before long my clients were asking me to come and help them get dressed on the day of the wedding. Whilst doing this I had what you would call a light bulb moment. I realised there was a career for me that would allow me to combine my creative and organisational skills. So whether your wedding is for 50 or 500 people. I am here to help you create a event that is sophisticated and unforgettable.
